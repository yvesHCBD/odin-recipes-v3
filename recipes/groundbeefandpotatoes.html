Ground Beef and Potatoes Recipe



Description: "This keema aloo curry is by far my favorite Indian recipe. Inspired by my mom, who still makes this for me to this day. If you want to try something a little different, I guarantee you will love this meal."

Ingredients:
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 extra-large onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons (Optional)
  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons grated fresh ginger root
  • 1 serrano chile pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 3 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup frozen green peas
  • 1 teaspoon garam masal
